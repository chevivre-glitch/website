About Black Hawk

Black Hawk writes high‑stakes dark fantasy and sci‑fantasy where haunted heroes grapple with impossible choices, ancient laws, and the thin edge between mercy and monstrosity. When not mapping empires and designing siege engines, Black Hawk is usually drafting songs, glyphs, and star‑charts that inevitably find their way into the stories.

Voice of the Dead Halo

He died. Then the world asked him to lead. Waking in a body that should have been a patch of corrupted memory, Valen finds himself bound to the fortress that once bore his name—and to a chorus of the dead who will not let him rest. To save what remains, he must master the rules that govern gods, break the ones that shackle mortals, and choose whether a broken crown is still worth wearing.

Genre: Dark Fantasy, LitRPG
